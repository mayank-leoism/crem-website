Frequently Asked Questions

Is Crem free to use?

Yes, Crem offers a free plan with all essential features. Premium features may be available in the future.

How is my data secured?

All your data is stored locally. We never collect any information.

Can I add multiple cards?

Absolutely! You can add and manage as many credit cards as you need.

Will my data be cleared if I re-install the app?

Yes, since all the data is stored locally, if you uninstall the app or clear data from settings you will lose all your data.

How do I backup my data?

We are currently working on this feature. It will be released with future version of app.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us via the contact form on our About page or email us at mayank.leoism@gmail.com.
